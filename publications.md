My publications span satellite climate data record development, tropical cyclone climatology, and satellite calibration methodologies. Below are some of my most highly cited contributions:

[**3,250+ citations**](https://doi.org/10.1175/2009BAMS2755.1) - *Foundational database unifying global tropical cyclone data for climate research*

### Globally Gridded Satellite Observations for Climate Studies (GridSat-B1 CDR)
